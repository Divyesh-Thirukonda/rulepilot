# RulePilot Terms of Use

RulePilot is a moderator utility for post triage. It provides classification suggestions, audit logs, and moderator workflow tools.

Moderators installing RulePilot are responsible for configuring it according to their community rules and applying their own judgment before final moderation decisions.

RulePilot does not guarantee perfect rule classification. False positives and false negatives are expected, especially for nuanced community rules.

RulePilot must not be used to harass users, profile users, surveil users, or make moderation decisions based on protected traits or private behavior.

RulePilot is provided as-is for hackathon and app review purposes.
